Become an Agent

Thank you for your interest in becoming a Houston General Insurance Independent Agent!

Agency Appointment Requirements

•Agent must possess and retain an active General Lines – Property & Casualty Agent or Personal Lines Agent in the State of Texas at all times.
•Agent must maintain professional liability insurance (E&O), in a coverage amount of $1,000,000 per claim.
•If applicable, Agent is required to disclose and list all aggregator/cluster affiliations on questionnaire.
•Agent must have a LexisNexis account to write business with HGIE.
